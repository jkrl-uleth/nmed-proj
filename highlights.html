Match Highlights

Anaconda Squeeze

One of Nacho's most famous moves is the Anaconda Squeeze. In this match with the Tall Boys he shouts at as he constricts on his opponent. This move is known to feel like it is bone crushing and it is difficult for opponents to recover.

Corn Slap

This move Nacho pulled in a tag team match against the duo Satan's Cavemen. This move shows how resourceful Nacho can be when he is fighting in the crowd. This was a turning point in the match, but unfortunately Esqueleto and Nacho could not get the win.

Ramses Finishing Move

This is Nacho's most famous wrestling move of all time. In his famous fight against Ramses, Nacho uses this beautiful pin after doing a massive dive to reach Ramses while he is on the defensive and running from the incoming Nacho. Nacho recovers from a foot on the neck in an impressive sequence leading up to the finishing blow.

Nipple Twist Backbreaker

Nacho pulled this move in the Battle Jam. The nipple twister is not a common move used in lucha libre, but it threw his opponent off so he could then follow it up with a second combo move. Nacho is not known to have used this move since the Battle Jam.
