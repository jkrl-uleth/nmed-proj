Who is Nacho Libre?

Bio

Nacho Libre is a luchador based out of Oaxaca, Mexico. Nacho has had a unique career because he originated as a monk in a monastery outside the city. Originally leading a double life because he was afraid of being shunned by the monastery, finally he went public with his career and got support from his peers. He donates the proceeds of his career to the orphanage attached to the monastery. Nacho came to fame recently when he beat the legendary Ramses as a massive underdog. Nacho's costume was hand crafted with materials around the moastery. His tag team partner, Esqueleto, he met in the city in Oaxaca, first meeting when they got into a street fight over some food!

Stats

  • Height: 5'6
  • Weight: 220 lbs
  • Ethnicity: Mexican/Scandinavian
